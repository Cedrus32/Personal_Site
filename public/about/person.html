    • TLDR: I go by Cedrus online. I started this site as a fun hobby to show off my other hobbies. =)

    Hey there, I'm Cedrus! I've dabbled in many hobbies over the years, but a few constants have always been video games, knitting, tamagotchis, and web dev. This site is a coalescing of these favorite hobbies into a single space online. A place to practice and show off what I love!

    My first video game memory was watching my dad play Doom on the family computer way back in the '90s. I eventually discovered my parents' Super Nintendo, and spent many hours playing Super Mario Bros, Star Fox, and A Link to the Past. Nowadays, I enjoy slow-paced, story-driven, and retro-inspired games.

    Also in the '90s, I received my first virtual pet -- a pink Komputer Koala GigaPet. I discovered and fell in love with Tamagotchis when the Connections first released in the early '00s, and I still love to play and collect the Connections today.

    I began dabbling in web dev in the early-mid '00s thanks to Neopets. From that, I fell into the world of pixel sharing and collecting. At one point I was even offered a hosted sub-domain in a pixel site ring. I maintained that site for a few years until life pulled me in other directions. Amazingly, I saved my pixels (adopt here) from that site!

    I started knitting in college as a de-stresser. Since then, I've successfully tackled a variety of garments for myself and loved ones -- scarves, socks, mittens, shawls, cowls, hats, and sweaters! I have a stash problem, and have gone on a self-imposed yarn buying ban. I'm currently on a journey to knit through my stash.

    Out of all the pages on the web, it's amazing you found this one. Thanks for taking the time to look around!

    All the best -- Cedrus
